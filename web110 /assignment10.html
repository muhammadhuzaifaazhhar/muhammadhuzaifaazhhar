Multimedia refers to communication that integrates various content forms such as text, audio, images, animations, and video into a single interactive presentation. It has evolved from traditional mass media, offering enhanced user engagement and interaction. The term was coined in the 1960s and has since encompassed a wide range of applications, including education, entertainment, journalism, engineering, and medicine. Examples include video games, online courses, virtual reality simulations, and augmented reality experiences. Multimedia facilitates learning, communication, and entertainment across diverse fields and platforms, contributing to a richer and more immersive user experience.!
